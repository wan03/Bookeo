# Project Requirements: Bookeo (DR Edition)

## Objective

To build a robust booking and business management platform tailored for the Dominican Republic market, connecting customers with beauty and grooming services through an engaging, TikTok-style discovery experience.

## Core Pillars
1.  **Business Centric:** Powerful portfolio management and marketing automation tools to help businesses grow.
2.  **Consumer Experience:** Intuitive discovery, booking, and interaction.
3.  **Trust & Engagement:** Verified purchase reviews, specifically focusing on **video reviews**.

## Key Constraints & Tech Stack
-   **Infrastructure:** Vercel (Frontend/Edge), Supabase (Backend/DB/Auth).
-   **Framework:** Next.js.
-   **Architecture:** Monolithic initially (Keep it simple).
-   **Location:** Dominican Republic (implies specific considerations for language, payments, and communication channels like WhatsApp).

## Feature Wishlist
-   **Marketing Automation:** Tools for businesses to engage customers (e.g., automated reminders, promos).
-   **Video Reviews:** Verified customers can leave video feedback.
-   **Portfolio:** High-quality showcase for business services.

## Documentation Strategy
-   Maintain thorough documentation of structure, technology choices, and progress.
-   Ensure the codebase is understandable for future maintenance.
